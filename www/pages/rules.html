Unlock-a-Gift

The Rules

  1. Open the packages in numerical order
  2. After you've opened a gift, return to the app
  3. For each gift there are four cards with information
  4. Some of the cards provide clues - it's up to you to figure out which ones
  5. Decipher the common theme between all five gifts - that will be your key
  6. Solve the puzzle by entering the key

Don't worry - if you get stuck, the app will help you -- but you're a mystery solving genius (thanks Murder She Wrote)!
