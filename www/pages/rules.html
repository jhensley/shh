Christmas Key

First, a few rules

  1. Open the packages in numerical order
  2. Only click the "Next Gift" after you've opened a new gift
  3. Some cards will reveal clues - figure out which are real
  4. Decipher the theme of the gifts - and you'll unlock the key
  5. When you think you're ready, click "Solve"

Don't worry - if you get stuck, the app will help you!
